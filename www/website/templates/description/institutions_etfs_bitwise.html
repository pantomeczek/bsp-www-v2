Bitwise Bitcoin Fund (BITB)

Founded in 2017, Bitwise Asset Management is a forefront provider in the crypto asset management field, known for introducing the first cryptocurrency index fund. Dedicated to offering accessible cryptocurrency exposure to investors, Bitwise emphasizes compliance, education, and sophisticated risk management. Its product line, including the notable Bitwise 10 Crypto Index Fund, is designed to capture the performance of selected cryptocurrencies through a meticulous, research-based selection process, aiming at potential returns and market diversification.
