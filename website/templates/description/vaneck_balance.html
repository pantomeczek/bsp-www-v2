VanEck Bitcoin Trust (HODL)

Founded in 1955 by John C. van Eck, VanEck is a renowned investment management firm, lauded for introducing exchange-traded funds (ETFs) and a variety of investment products across multiple asset classes. VanEck is distinguished for its emphasis on gold funds, international markets, and pioneering investments in digital assets. Offering ETFs, mutual funds, and institutional accounts, VanEck seeks to deliver strategic investment options in commodities, natural resources, emerging technologies, and global equities, underpinned by comprehensive research and a vision for growth sectors.
